# Shop-Sales-and-Profit-Report
This report provides an overall view of shop performance by analyzing sales, profit, and product trends. It helps stakeholders track key business metrics, identify high-performing products, and understand customer purchasing behavior to support data-driven decisions.

# Key KPIs 
•Total Sales – Overall revenue generated
•Total Profit – Net earnings after costs
•Profit Margin (%) – Profitability efficiency
•Total Quantity Sold – Volume of products sold
•Average Order Value (AOV) – Average revenue per order
•Number of Orders / Customers – Business reach and demand

# Key Insights
•Identifies top-selling and most profitable products
•Highlights loss-making or low-profit items
•Shows sales and profit trends over time
•Compares category-wise or segment-wise performance
•Helps detect high sales but low profit scenarios
•Reveals customer buying patterns and demand trends

•Bar / Column Chart
-Compares sales or profit across products, categories, or regions
-Easily identifies top and bottom performers

•Line Chart
-Shows sales and profit trends over time
-Helps track growth, seasonality, or decline

•Pie / Donut Chart
-Displays contribution of categories or segments to total sales/profit

•Scatter Chart
-Shows relationship between Sales vs Quantity or Profit
-Helps identify high-volume low-profit or premium products

•Table / Matrix
-Provides detailed breakdown of sales, profit, and quantity
-Useful for granular analysis
